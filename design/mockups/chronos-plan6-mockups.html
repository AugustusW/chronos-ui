v0.1.0 · crontab

Bring order to your schedules

ChronosUI reads the jobs already in your native scheduler — your crontab. Nothing is moved or migrated.

Scan to see every cron line as an editable job, with run history once you adopt it. Or create a brand-new scheduled job from scratch.

Reading crontab -l — read-only until you choose to adopt or edit a job.
